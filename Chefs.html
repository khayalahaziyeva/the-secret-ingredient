Tahir Amiraslanov

    Awards:

Work Experience:


He has been a member of the International Organization of Folk Art of UNESCO since 1998, Deputy Chairman of the Committee on Traditional Food since 2000, and Chairman since 2004.

He is the vice-president of the Russian Culinary Association and a member of the World Board of Judges.

He is an honorary member of the French Culinary Academy, the Culinary Associations of Malta, Romania, Jerusalem, the UAE and other countries.

He is the president of the Azerbaijan National Culinary Association.

Since 1998, T. Amiraslanov has represented Azerbaijan in the jury of world competitions.

...

In 1992, thanks to him, Azerbaijan became the first country to be admitted to the World Union of Culinary Organizations (WCO) from the former USSR, the Turkic and Islamic worlds. Then Russia, Turkey, Kazakhstan, Serbia, Belarus, Moldova, Uzbekistan, Kyrgyzstan and others. countries became members of the WTO with its active assistance. Elected Honorary Member of the UNDP.

In 2017, he was elected Vice President of the World Islamic Culinary Association.

He is a board member and ambassador of the World Gatronomy Institute.

French National Culinary Academy, Sicilian Culinary Academy, etc. He is an honorary member. He is an academician of the International Academy of Sciences.

He is an Academician of the Georgian Academy of Sciences.

He is the founder of the new Informative food theory.

In 1993, he was awarded the title of Master Chef by the Ministry of Commerce. Shahhuseyn Karimov was awarded this honorary title not after the VI degree, but after the IV degree for his knowledge and skills in the field of cooking.

In 1997, Tahir Amiraslanov dedicated an article to him in the magazine Pitaniya and Obshchestvo, published in Russia with a circulation of one million, and called him the King of Azerbaijani cuisine. Later it remains a pseudonym.

Over the years, AMKA and AMKA have been sent to events in Singapore, Malaysia, Malta, France, Germany, Belgium, America, Italy, Turkey, Russia, UAE, China and other countries.

He received many awards and was awarded gold medals.

The "Star of the 3rd Millennium" competition of the Russian Culinary Association, held in Moscow in 2000 among more than 3,000 chefs, is hosted by the Vice President of the Russian Culinary Association Tahir Amiraslanov.
